Setup Instructions for Auto Invoice Generator

For the auto invoice generator I'll have a downloaded copy of my make.com scenario. I'll send it over to your gmail and you'll download it and import it into your scenario. We'll change the webhook so you can have your own personalized website and we can also change how the invoice form looks. You'll have to make a spreadsheet that's connected your gmail account (also works for storing data). You'll also have to make a google doc that has the layout of a invoice (can be personalized). Then your business email will send a invoice to you and your customer.
